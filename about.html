About HVG Partners

HVG Partners is a company implementing innovative vending machines in the Greek market. We offer an exclusive solution for businesses: an additional source of income and increased customer flow through the installation of autonomous perfume vending machines.

Why is this profitable for businesses in Greece?

Greece is a country with high tourist activity, a strong restaurant culture and many points of attraction. In such locations, perfume vending machines become a natural part of service: they offer guests a fast mini‑purchase, create emotional interest and stimulate repeated interactions.

For businesses this means increased revenue, higher customer loyalty and a modern level of service without staff involvement.
